**CAR**

**Attributes**
* amountOfGas (integer)
* paintColor (string)
* passengerSeats (integer)
* engineOn (boolean)

**Methods**
* changePassengerSeat (adds or removes passengerSeats)
* paintCar (changes paintColor attribute)
* fillGasTank (increases amountOfGas)
* turnEngineOff (changes engineOn boolean)
